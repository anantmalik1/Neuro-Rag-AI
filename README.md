## 🌟 About NeuroRAG AI

<img align="right" src="https://media.giphy.com/media/du3J3cXyzhj75IOgvA/giphy.gif" width="280"/>

**NeuroRAG AI** is a cutting-edge **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** system that combines the power of large language models with intelligent document retrieval.

🎯 Upload your documents and ask questions — get **accurate, context-aware answers in real-time**.

> 💡 *"Bringing the future of conversational AI to your fingertips."*

### ✨ What makes it special?

- 🎯 **Highly Accurate** — Context-aware responses backed by your documents
- ⚡ **Lightning Fast** — Optimized retrieval pipeline
- 🌐 **Multi-format Support** — PDF, TXT, DOCX, and more
- 🔒 **Privacy First** — Your data stays secure
- 🎨 **Beautiful UI** — Modern Gradio interface
